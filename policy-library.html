Relevant Government of Canada policies and policy intstruments

This section is specifically concerned with ensuring that HR initiatives comply with Canadian labor laws, privacy regulations like PIPEDA, and other relevant guidelines. Here's how this section aligns with relevant Government of Canada policies and policy instruments:

 

Key Aspects Covered:

  • Compliance with Canadian labor laws

  • Adherence to privacy regulations such as PIPEDA

  • Alignment with public sector-specific guidelines

  • Proactive measures for maintaining compliance with legal and regulatory frameworks

  • Regular compliance audits and checks

 

Relevant Government of Canada Policies and Policy Instruments:

 

1. Policy on Privacy Protection:

  • This policy guides the handling of personal information within government operations, ensuring that such data is managed according to legal and policy requirements, specifically aligning with the principles of PIPEDA. Policy on Privacy Protection- Canada.ca

 

2. Canada Labour Code:

  • Governs the rights and responsibilities of employers and employees in Canada. It establishes standards for fair treatment, including hours of work, wages, leaves, and dispute resolution. Canada Labour Code (justice.gc.ca)

 

3. Directive on Privacy Practices:

  • Helps ensure that the privacy rights of individuals are protected in programs and activities involving the handling of personal information by government institutions. Directive on Privacy Practices- Canada.ca

 

4. Directive on the Administration of the Access to Information Act:

  • Ensures that government information is available to the public while also respecting privacy rights, which is essential when HR initiatives involve personal employee data. Directive on Privacy Practices- Canada.ca

 

5. Treasury Board Secretariat Guidelines on Ethics and Compliance:

  • These provide a framework for ensuring ethical behavior and compliance in government operations, which includes maintaining compliance with all applicable regulations and laws.

  1. Values and Ethics Code for the Public Sector: This code outlines the values and expected ethical behaviors for public servants, including compliance with laws and policies. Values and Ethics Code for the Public Sector- Canada.ca

  2. Policy on People Management: 

    1. Section 3, 4. and 6 of this policy emphasize the importance of upholding ethical values and ensuring compliance with relevant legislation, regulations, and policies. Policy on People Management- Canada.ca

  3. Framework for the Management of Compliance

    1. This document talks about how the government manages adherence to rules and regulations. It emphasizes shared responsibility between TBS and public servants for understanding and following the rules. Framework for the Management of Compliance- Canada.ca

These policies and directives ensure that any HR-related initiatives within the core public administration of Canada not only comply with the necessary legal frameworks but also uphold the highest standards of ethics and privacy protection.
